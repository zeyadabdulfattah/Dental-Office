A Minimally Invasive Smile Makeover

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin shells of ceramic material bonded to the front of teeth. This transformative procedure can be an ideal solution for improving your smile and can conceal a variety of dental imperfections.

  • Correct Multiple Flaws: Instantly fix chips, cracks, gaps, and severe discoloration.
  • Achieve Natural Beauty: High-quality porcelain mimics the light-reflecting properties of natural teeth.
  • Durable & Stain-Resistant: Enjoy a beautiful, white smile for years to come.
  • Conservative Approach: Veneers require minimal removal of natural tooth structure.

How It Works: The Path to Perfection

Our smile design process is a collaborative art, ensuring your final result is uniquely you.

Smile Design & Consultation

This is where we co-design your perfect smile. We discuss your goals, take digital photos, and create a "digital wax-up" so you can preview your new look before we even begin.

Tooth Preparation

A very thin layer of enamel is gently removed from the tooth surface to make room for the veneer. We then take a precise digital impression for our master ceramist.

Temporary Veneers & Crafting

You'll leave our office with beautiful temporary veneers while your custom porcelain veneers are meticulously hand-crafted by our dental laboratory partner.

Final Bonding

At your final appointment, we'll try in your custom veneers, make any final adjustments, and then permanently bond them to your teeth, revealing your stunning new smile.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my porcelain veneers?

Caring for veneers is simple: treat them as you would your natural teeth. Maintain good oral hygiene by brushing and flossing daily, and attend your regular dental check-ups and cleanings.

Are veneers permanent?

Veneers are considered a permanent treatment because a small amount of enamel is removed to place them. However, the veneers themselves can last 10-20 years or more with proper care before they may need to be replaced.

Will my veneers look fake?

Absolutely not. We work with world-class ceramists who use multiple layers of high-quality porcelain to create a natural translucency and appearance. We meticulously match the shade and shape to complement your facial features for a result that is beautiful, not artificial.
