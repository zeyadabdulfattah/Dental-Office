The Cornerstone of Oral Health

Preventive care is the most important service we provide. Regular professional cleanings and check-ups are essential for maintaining healthy teeth and gums, preventing complex issues before they start, and keeping your smile looking its best.

  • Prevent Cavities & Gum Disease: Remove plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing can't reach.
  • Early Detection of Issues: Identify potential problems like oral cancer, decay, or failing restorations early.
  • Maintain Fresh Breath: Professional cleaning helps eliminate bacteria that cause persistent bad breath.
  • Protect Your Overall Health: Oral health is directly linked to systemic health, including heart disease and diabetes.

How It Works: The Professional Hygiene Visit

Our hygiene appointments are thorough, gentle, and tailored to you.

Comprehensive Examination

Our dentist and hygienist perform a thorough check of your teeth, gums, and entire mouth to screen for any potential issues.

Gentle Scaling & Polishing

Our skilled hygienists use advanced tools to gently remove hardened plaque (tartar) and then polish your teeth to remove surface stains, leaving them smooth and bright.

Expert Flossing & Rinsing

We finish with a professional flossing and rinse to ensure every surface is perfectly clean and fresh.

Personalized Home Care Plan

We provide you with personalized advice and techniques to help you maintain your beautiful smile between visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a professional cleaning?

For most patients, we recommend a professional cleaning and check-up every six months. Some patients, especially those with a history of gum disease, may benefit from more frequent visits.

Is teeth whitening safe for my enamel?

Yes, when performed under professional supervision, teeth whitening is very safe. The materials we use are approved and have been proven not to damage tooth enamel. We ensure your gums are protected during in-office procedures.

How long do teeth whitening results last?

Results can last for several years, depending on your diet and lifestyle. Avoiding staining foods and drinks like coffee, tea, and red wine will help maintain your bright smile. We also offer touch-up kits for our take-home whitening patients.
